Fresh Egg Noodle Pasta from Scratch

This simple recipe is easy, fast, and delicious. Fresh pasta has a much richer texture and flavor than store-bought dry pasta.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups semolina flour
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt

Directions

  1. Add the flour to a large mixing bowl and create a well in the center.
  2. Add the eggs, salt, and oil to well.
  3. Slowly mix the ingredients until a shaggy dough forms.
  4. Knead the dough for 5 minutes or until smooth.
  5. Rest the dough for at least 30 minutes.
  6. Roll out the dough to roughly 1/8 inch in thickness.
  7. Using a knife or pasta cutter, cut the dough into strips.
  8. Boil pasta for roughly 3 minutes or until al' dente.
  9. Serve hot in your choice of sauce.
