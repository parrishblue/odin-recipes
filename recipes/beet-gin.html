Golden Beet Gin

Golden Beet Infused Gin

Golden Beet Gin is fantastic for fall cocktails. It has a beautiful amber hue and a mellow, sweet, earthy flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 750ml bottle of neutral, dry gin
  • 3 medium golden beets, as fresh as possible

Directions

  1. Peel the beets.
  2. Using a mandolin, slice the beets into strips as thinly as possible.
  3. Place the beet chips in a dehydrator on a rack so they do not overlap.
  4. Remove the beet chips when fully dry and crisp.
  5. Place half the beet chips and half the gin in a high quality whipped cream canister.
  6. Insert and discharge one nitrogen canister.
  7. Let sit for 5 minutes to allow for infusion.
  8. De-pressurize and strain the gin through cheese cloth. It should have a deep and vibrant yellow hue.
  9. Repeat process with 2nd half of beets and gin.
  10. Store in a refrigerated, closed vessel to prevent oxidation.
